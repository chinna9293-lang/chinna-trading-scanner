📈 Chart Indicators Explained:
EMA9 = Fast moving average (green line) - Shows short-term trend direction
EMA21 = Slow moving average (orange line) - Confirms longer-term trend
Support/Resistance = Dashed lines showing price levels where reversals may occur
TP (Green dashed) = Take Profit target for buy signals
SL (Red dashed) = Stop Loss level to protect your position
📊 Signal Indicators:
RSI (Relative Strength Index) = Momentum indicator (0-100 scale)
RSI below 37 = Oversold (Potential BUY)
RSI 37-63 = Neutral (Consolidating)
RSI above 63 = Overbought (Potential SELL)
🎯 Trading Direction Guide:
↑ BULLISH = EMA9 above EMA21, strong uptrend signal
↓ BEARISH = EMA9 below EMA21, strong downtrend signal
→ NEUTRAL = EMAs close together, no clear trend (waiting phase)
